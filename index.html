Welcome to my Website

On this website you will get to know two of my favorite foods.

Pizza

Pizza is a fantastic food choice and my personal favorite to eat as it can be customized in a versatile way. While the main component of a pizza is the dough, the sauces and toppings can be interchangeable and versatile depending on one’s preferences. The crust can be thin, thick, or even be filled with cheese if that’s what your heart desires.

Traditional flatbreads/pizza was first documented by the name “pizza” in 997 CE from southern Italy. But some other sources say flatbread originated in China, so it’s unknown where pizza truly originated from. An interesting fact is that October is national pizza month.

Tacos

Similar to pizza, tacos are also another versatile food that I enjoy. Not only are they delicious, but the amount of toppings and fillings (meat, fish, etc) that can interchangeable with just a corn tortilla is amazing.

The word “taco” is said to be originated in the 18th century, where Mexican miners rolled Gunpowder in a paper like a “taquito” to denote rocks. But actual “tacos” were said to be invited by the ancient Mexican civilization where they made flat corn tortilla and filled them with fish, organs, and other meats. October also seems to have a national day for tacos, that being October 4th.
